Frequently Asked Questions

What is covered under a home warranty?

Home warranties cover major systems and appliances including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, kitchen appliances, and more. Coverage varies by plan.

How quickly can I file a claim?

You can file a claim 24/7 through our online portal or by calling our support team. Most claims are processed within 24 hours.

Are there any deductibles?

Most plans include a small service fee per claim, typically ranging from $75-$125. This is much less than paying for repairs out of pocket.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your protection plan at any time. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new plans.
