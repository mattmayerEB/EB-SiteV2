Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday from 8AM-8PM EST and Saturday-Sunday from 9AM-5PM EST. Emergency claims support is available 24/7.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during business days. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

Can I schedule a callback?

Yes! When you call us, you can request a callback at a more convenient time. We'll call you back within the hour during business hours.

Do you have multilingual support?

Yes, we have customer service representatives who speak English, Spanish, and French. Let us know your preferred language when you contact us.

How can I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information by calling us, sending an email, or logging into your online account if you have one.

Is there a fee for customer service calls?

No, all customer service calls are free. We're here to help you with any questions or concerns about your protection plan.
