Claims Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a claim?

Most claims are reviewed and approved within 24 hours. Emergency claims for home systems are processed immediately.

What information do I need to file a claim?

You'll need your policy number, description of the problem, and photos of the damaged item if applicable.

Is there a deductible for claims?

Yes, most plans have a small service fee (typically $25-$100)

What if my item can't be repaired?

If an item cannot be repaired, we will assist with replacement of a similar model.

Can I choose my own repair technician?

We work with a network of pre-screened, licensed technicians. You can request a specific provider if you have a preference.
