FAQ

Most frequent questions and answers

HOW DO I FILE A REPAIR SERVICE REQUEST IF SOMETHING BREAKS?

To file a service request, call 888-994-0914 to request service on any one of your eligible products or visit us online and file a request through your customer portal.

DO I NEED TO KEEP MY RECEIPTS?

It's always a good idea to keep receipts for your covered items, but they're not always required for claims processing.

WHO PERFORMS THE REPAIR SERVICE IF SOMETHING BREAKS?

We work with a network of certified, licensed technicians who will perform the repair or replacement service for your covered items.

IS THERE A SERVICE CHARGE FOR APPLIANCE AND SYSTEMS REPAIRS?

Yes, there is a service charge that you pay directly to the technician. The amount varies by plan and type of service.

CAN I CANCEL MY PROTECTION PLAN?

Yes, you can cancel your protection plan at any time. Please contact our customer service team for assistance with cancellation.
