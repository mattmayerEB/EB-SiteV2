Protecting What Matters Most

Everything Breaks has been providing comprehensive protection plans for homes, electronics, and vehicles since 2015. We understand that when things break, you need fast, reliable service to get back to normal.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of insurance and technology experts, Everything Breaks was born from a simple observation: traditional insurance often falls short when you need it most. We set out to create protection plans that actually work when things go wrong.

Our founders, having experienced the frustration of broken appliances, electronics, and home systems, knew there had to be a better way. They envisioned a service that would be there when you need it, with transparent pricing, fast response times, and quality repairs.

Today, we've helped over 500,000 families across the United States protect their most important possessions. From smartphones to HVAC systems, from laptops to automobiles, we're there when everything breaks.
